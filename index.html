Who We Are

We Promote Open Source. We Build Open Source. We Teach Open Source.

Network for the Open Web was founded to serve as a platform for Internet Education. We strive to protect and teach people about the uses of the open web.

What We Do

Our Ongoing Projects

We are on a mission to build and innovate on open source projects that keep the web open.

Netco

App to simplify recruitment process. A user friendly android app for de-cluttering the process of recruiting individuals for a specific role in the various college committees.

Salute

A secure messaging app. An application that provides you to communicate with someone without having the fear of your privacy on your mind

TunePref

Finding tunes to your groove. An application developed using Machine Learning that helps you find the best tunes that suits your preference.

Our Events

Take a look at the various events conducted by our club

HacktoberFest'18

View image

A month-long celebration of the Open Web Culture with challenging events and exciting prices for participants. Also a Crash course on how people can contribute to ongoing open-source projects on GitHub.

Android Workshop

View image

N.O.W. collaborated with the Department of Computer Engineering to organize the Android Competiton for the students. With an audience of over 200 students, our skills were put to the test as this was the first major event NOW was a part of. The team was brilliant and showed amazing coherence.
